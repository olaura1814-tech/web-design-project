About Me

Eve Omondi is a Records and Archives Management consultant with extensive experience in designing, implementing, and evaluating information management systems for both public and private sector organizations. She has supported institutions in developing effective records management policies, classification schemes, and digital preservation frameworks to enhance accountability, compliance, and service delivery. Her work focuses on ensuring the proper creation, maintenance, and preservation of records throughout their lifecycle, with a strong emphasis on digital transformation, disaster recovery, and business continuity. As a consultant, Eve has helped organizations streamline filing systems, improve retrieval efficiency, and align records practices with national laws and international standards.
